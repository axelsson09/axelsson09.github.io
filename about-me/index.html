About Me

👋 Hey, I'm Axelsson09!

🎮 Gamer | 💻 Programmer | 🧠 Creative Thinker | 🕹️ Founder of Gamemodstudios

I'm William Axelsson, better known online as Axelsson09. I'm a passionate developer and tech enthusiast based in Sweden, born in 2009. My journey began with game modding, and at just 14, I launched Gamemodstudios — a studio dedicated to immersive, moddable experiences.

Gamemodstudios thrives on collaboration, creativity, and user empowerment. Our core belief? The best games invite the community to shape them. I'm here to make that easier and more exciting.

When I'm not coding or designing, you’ll find me testing new tools, innovating with the team, or engaging with our awesome modding community.

Tech Stack: Unity, C#, Java, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Ionic, Git, Blender, Supabase, and more.

“Start with curiosity, build with passion, and always leave room for play.”
